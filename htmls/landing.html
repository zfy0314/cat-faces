Welcome to an online study on human memory

This study is for a course project in 85-211 Cognitive Psychology at Carnegie Mellon University.

There will be no compensation nor cost to participate in this study; participantion is completely voluntary.

If you choose to participate, you would first need to complete a background survey. No private information will be recorded. Afterwards you will be shown a short video of 12 cartoon cats. Each cat will be shown for 3 seconds, separated by 0.5 second of blank screen You should try your best to remember all of them. After the video, you will be given 30s to recall all the cats you have seen. Then you will watch a educational video on inattentional blindness. And finally you will complete a short task related to the cats.

The study will take around 5-10 minutes

By clicking the start below, you consent that you are at least 18 years old and agree to participate in the study
